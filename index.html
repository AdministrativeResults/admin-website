History of the AR64A

The AR64A Rig is based on the South African 32 Battalion H-Harness that saw wide usage in the later Cold War years between 1969-1989. The original SDF 32 Battalion H-Harness has been a staple of Administrative Results from the very beginning. It is our privilege to bring to life a completely enhanced version of this awesome piece of equipment in Ranger Green and Multicam.
