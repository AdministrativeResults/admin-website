Shipping Policy

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days. We offer free ground shipping on all orders over $150 within the continental U.S. Expedited options (2-day or overnight) are available at checkout for an additional fee.

You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information as soon as your order leaves our warehouse. If you have any questions about your shipment, please email shipping@3fsupply.com.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you need to return, you may do so within 30 days of delivery. Returned items must be in new, unused condition with all original tags attached.

To start a return, please email returns@3fsupply.com with your order number and reason for return. Once approved, we will provide a prepaid shipping label. Refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the return.
